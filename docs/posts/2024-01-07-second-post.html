Music

Maybe I told you about this, I was kind of a musician when young. I did play the cornet, which is a similar instrument to the trumpet but without the ventiles for air. It was funny because in Mexico, it is common to have music bands to perform civic ceremonies that honoured the flag etc. So the music played is more related to the military routines that honor the nations symbols. The songs are more like melodies that serve to give orders during parades. It was a funny time because I did it during the university to gain some sports credits and this activity was included. I was attracted to the discipline and rigid aspect of the order of the band. The music itself has its own charm. Here I leave you a video of a band performing some bands, similar to what I did as well.

Well, the idea is that knowing how to play the cornet, learning to play the trumpet should not be that difficult. And one very nice thing is that Mariachis, the Mexican typical band of regional music, also needs trumpets. Here in Munich there is one nice band that usually plays in the Mexican events, making the parties always happier. And since this year, I got the invitation of one guy working in the band to play with them :) Of course, I need to learn a decent repertoire of songs, lets say 5 or 10 for a beginning. But then I would be able to play with them, which could serve as a very funny hobby as musician. We could play in the many Mexican parties here in Munich, ir in different other events as weddings or other ceremonies. Being a Mariachi has always been a funny project for me and now it seems to be not so far away. I even have now a trumpet which I bought for a very fair price before leaving for holidays. So practice is what should come next :) Here you have some videos of various successful artists playing very famous Mexican songs with Mariachi.
