Creando puentes

Published on December 29, 2023

"Creando puentes" literally means "building bridges".

"El puente de Metlac" | José María Velasco, 1881

¿Porqué un puente?

I came across the idea of this blog as an opportunity to build a bridge that will allow us to establish communication. Many things were explained and clarified from my side in our last "long" converstions by chat. I described many ideas and I did a summary of the events that I considered important regarding the time development after our separation. I also wrote it with very much frustation and perhaps you could also felt that while reading it, for that I apologize.

But I consider it was important for you to see that I consider there is a logic in how I have been approaching to you, that there is an understanding of the mistakes I consider I did and most important, I assume my responsibility and the consequences of the errors I have done.

A bridge then is a way in how a new form of communication can happen. A new communication that shall be between us, Hana, because after all what we have said to each other, it is very clear how much I care about about you and how much feelings are still present inside me, and maybe inside of you as well. Perhaps, a bit of watering can awake the seed.

How achieve this?

I can be present here for you, to receive and listen your ideas and thoughts as well as way in which we could start a new type of relationship, maybe more as friends than lovers because we are not in the position to ask and give love in the distance and withouth healing what happen before between us. But to awake the love and create a better, more mature and confident love relation is my goal and I am very aware it requires strong foundations.

I am aware I cannot ask you a relationship, especially in the distance situation we are right now and the lack of future plans from both of us. But while communicating, new paths can become clear to us and if we coincide again then we may walk again together hand to hand.

Speaking of bridges, here I am next to one

Actualidad

Pues ahora mismo, yo estoy de hecho bastante ocupado. Mañana mismo tengo un viaje al norte de Italia. Tengo la visita de mi familia aqui en casa y es para mi muy importante mostrarles algunos lugares que considero muy especiales aqui en Europa. Ya hemos viajado un poco en las semanas anteriores, pero aún tenemos mucho que ver en el futuro.

El viaje de mañana será solo una pequena forma de progresar en el objetivo mayor. Regresaré a inicios de año y te escribiré entonces algunas nuevas ideas que ya tengo en la mente y te haré saber cómo ha ido en este viaje para nosotros.

Espero que puedas encontrar la motivacion y la voluntad de hacerme saber tambien algo de ti.

¿Cómo te encuentras?

¿Qué sentiste al ver los glaciares en Argentina y cómo terminarás el año?

Me haría muy feliz saber algo de ti, querida Hana.
