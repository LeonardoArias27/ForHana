Creando puentes

Published on December 29, 2023

"Creando puentes" literally means "building bridges".

"El puente de Metlac" | José María Velasco, 1881

¿Porqué un puente?

Dear Hana, I came across the idea of this blog as an opportunity to build a bridge that will allow us to establish communication. Many things were explained and clarified from my side in our last "long" converstions by chat. I described many ideas and I did a summary of the events that I considered important regarding the time development after our separation. If you read that message with calm, perhaps you find a bit of sense and coherence, especially if you pay attention to the dates. I also wrote it with some frustation in our exchange of "what I did wrong" and "what you did wrong", perhaps you could also feel that from my side while reading it. For that I apologize since I never meant to be mean or offensive but clear.

I consider it was important for you to see that I consider there is a logic in how I have been approaching to you, that there is an understanding of the mistakes I did and most important, I assume my responsibility and the consequences of the errors I have done, which now gives me a better perspective to start to build something again for us, because I believe it is possible still.

A bridge is a way or form that allows communication can to happen or flow. A new communication that shall be between us, Hana, because after all what we have said to each other, I intend it is very clear how much I care about about you and how many feelings are still present inside me, and maybe inside of you as well. Perhaps, a bit of watering can awake the seed.

How to achieve this?

Here, I will be present for you, to share, provide and communicate my ideas and thoughts for I aspire to develop a new type of relation between us, maybe more as friends than lovers as you said to me before, because we are not in the position to ask and give love in the distance and withouth healing what happen before between us.

But now you see that it is my goal to awake the love and create a better, more mature and confident relation. Such relation requires strong fundations and communication is perhaps one of the most important because while communicating, new paths can become open and if we both coincide in them again, then we may walk again together again, hand to hand.

Speaking of bridges, here I am next to one

Actualidad

Ahora mismo yo estoy con mucho que hacer aqui en mi cotidianidad. Mañana mismo tengo un viaje al norte de Italia. Tengo la visita de mi familia aqui en casa y es para mi muy importante mostrarles algunos lugares que considero muy especiales aqui en Europa. Ya hemos viajado un poco en las semanas anteriores, pero aún tenemos mucho que ver en el futuro.

El viaje de mañana será solo una pequena forma de progresar en el objetivo mayor. Regresaré a inicios de año y te escribiré entonces algunas nuevas ideas que ya tengo en la mente y te haré saber cómo ha ido en este viaje para nosotros.

Espero que puedas encontrar la motivacion y la voluntad de hacerme saber tambien algo de ti.

¿Cómo te encuentras?

¿Qué sentiste al ver los glaciares en Argentina y cómo terminarás el año?

Me haría muy feliz saber algo de ti, querida Hana.
